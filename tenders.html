About

Tenders in FLAMS-Bridge are special purchase requests created by industries for bulk buying of agricultural products. These tenders specify the product type, required quantity, quality standards, and delivery timeline. Once a tender is posted, the FLAMS team evaluates and forwards it to eligible farmers. Farmers then produce and supply the goods as per the tender terms. This system ensures organized large-scale transactions and benefits both industries and farmers through planned production and assured sales.
